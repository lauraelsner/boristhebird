Boris the Bird

Boris is a Quaker or "Monk" Parakeet. Monk parakeets are highly intelligent, social birds. Those kept as pets routinely develop large vocabularies. They are able to learn scores of words and phrases. Due to this early speaking ability, it is overtaking the cockatiel as the favorite bird to teach to talk. Another asset is that this bird has a much more reasonable life span and price than African grey parrots or the yellow-naped amazon.
